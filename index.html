About T-Viewer
T-Viewer is a companion app for LSP D-Planner — a technical dive decompression planner.

Use T-Viewer to open, read, and share the dive plan text files exported by LSP D-Planner directly on your Android device. Supports syntax highlighting for deco schedules, depth data, warnings, and gas labels.

Features:
  • Open .txt / .log / .plan / .div files
  • Receive shared files from LSP D-Planner
  • Syntax highlighting for deco logs
  • Dark & light theme (tap 🌙 / ☀️)
  • Pinch-to-zoom + font size slider
  • Copy & share text
  • Drag & drop on desktop browser
